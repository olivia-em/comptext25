Catullus 85

Odi et amo. Quare id faciam, fortasse requiris?
Nescio, sed fierei sentio et excrucior.



Ezra Pound

I hate and I love. Why? You may ask but
It beats me. I feel it done to me, and ache.



Cid Corman

Hate: and I love. Why? Who knows why?
Nothing I say chokes the ache.



Olivia Lee

My hate is love, and you might wonder why.
I don't know, but I feel it happening and I'm crucified.
